Personalised digital healthcare app

Indonesia is committed to improving general access to healthcare by driving digitalisation in this sector through innovation projects and better networking of the health system. In this context, one of Indonesia's largest healthcare providers aimed to develop a revolutionary solution: Indonesia's first digital health app that is perfectly tailored to the individual needs and lifestyles of its users.

The solution should be designed not only to enable users to arrange medical online services but also to offer advice on preventive care and provide information for a healthier lifestyle.

Problem Statement

CHALLENGE

The client needed a single platform that bridges online care and in-clinic services, giving patients seamless access to booking, consultation and results in one digital workflow.

GOAL

Create a one-stop health app for bookings, consults and preventive-care guidance that anchors their digital ecosystem.

OUTCOMES AND KEY TAKEAWAYS

  • Indonesia’s first all-in-one health app went live, widening access to care across the country.

  • The clinician companion app launched on Google Play with 5 k+ installs

  • Our integration architecture linked patient, doctor and back-office tools into a single data flow, proving our expertise in building a connected app ecosystem.

  • Requirements cleared first review, so development kicked off ahead of schedule.

  • A streamlined design system kept the UI consistent and sprint velocity high.

  • User-centred design met business targets and real-world patient needs, strengthening Prodia’s digital healthcare platform.

Design Library

Challenges with the current design library

  • Many XD files, no single source of truth

  • Repeated colours, text and spacing; “official” styles unclear

  • Ad-hoc components without variants; updates didn’t cascade

  • Inconsistent naming wrecked search

  • Unused artboards bloated the file and slowed XD

  • No proper hand-off specs

  • Bahasa handled by full-screen duplicates, multiplying debt

How we fixed it

  • Held a hand-over call with the previous designer to walk through the XD files before starting the audit.

  • Merged all designs into one Cloud XD master file.

  • Deduped colours, text and spacing into a single asset library.

  • Rebuilt components with states so updates cascade.

  • Applied clear naming rules and bulk-renamed layers.

  • Published one-click Design Specs for dev hand-off.

  • Streamlined Bahasa localisation with tracked changes and an Excel glossary.
