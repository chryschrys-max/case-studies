Scoring rubric (1–5 scale):
5 = Strong/healthy — habit consistently maintained, supported by structures
4 = Good — mostly maintained with minor lapses
3 = Mixed — inconsistent, depends on context and day
2 = Declining — more bad days than good, system supports weakening
1 = Poor — habit largely abandoned or severely disrupted

Evidence basis: Each score draws on multiple participant accounts describing behaviours at that developmental stage. Where fewer than 3 participants provide evidence for a stage, scores are marked with ≈ to indicate lower confidence.
