What I do

Where I operate

Strategy & Problem Framing

Opportunity assessment, stakeholder alignment, business case development, and strategic roadmaps. I scope engagements from pre-sales through discovery — shaping the brief before shaping the solution.

Discovery & Research

Ethnographic fieldwork, contextual inquiry, behavioural research, service blueprinting, and synthesis frameworks. I run rolling analysis against hypotheses — adjusting focus as patterns emerge, not waiting for end-of-project reports.

Product Direction & Delivery

From information architecture and prototyping through to backlog shaping, design-engineering collaboration, and design systems governance. I stay involved through delivery to make sure what ships matches what was designed.

Team & Capability Building

Hiring, mentoring, maturity assessments, and building design operations. I’ve built practice communities across offices and mentored designers into senior roles — embedding ways of working that outlast individual projects.

Industries: Government & Public Sector, Healthcare & Life Sciences, Financial Services, B2B SaaS, Consumer Platforms. Comfortable in regulated environments, legacy modernisation programmes, and multi-stakeholder settings where alignment is as important as the solution itself.

Services

I turn ambiguous problems into clear direction — through research, design, and strategy.

Discovery Sprint

4–6 weeks

Most teams skip straight to building. This is the work that happens before that — so you’re solving the right problem, not just shipping fast.

I run a focused discovery: stakeholder interviews, user research, behavioural analysis, and synthesis into clear strategic recommendations. You walk away knowing what to build, why, and for whom.

You get

  • Research findings and synthesis
  • Opportunity areas and prioritised recommendations
  • Journey maps or service blueprints where relevant
  • A clear brief for whatever comes next

Good for: healthcare orgs, fintechs, startups, and government-adjacent teams who need clarity before committing to a direction.

UX/Design Team Audit & Capability Building

4–8 weeks

Your design team exists. But is it working at the level your product needs?

I assess where your team actually is — skills, processes, cross-functional collaboration — and build a clear path forward. Not abstract theory. Practical frameworks tied to real project work.

You get

  • Design maturity assessment
  • Individual growth plans per designer
  • Process and workflow redesign
  • Design ops recommendations
  • Training programme tied to live delivery

Good for: BPOs, fintechs, and mid-size tech companies building or scaling internal design functions.

Fractional Design & Research

Ongoing, part-time, embedded

Need senior design or research capability without the full-time headcount? I embed into your team on a flexible basis — as a design lead, hands-on UX designer, researcher, or CX consultant — depending on where the gap is.

Depending on what you need, I can

  • Lead product and experience direction
  • Run hands-on UX and interaction design
  • Own research and synthesis end-to-end
  • Map and redesign customer and service experiences

Good for: teams between hires, scaling fast, or running a specific programme that needs experienced design input.
